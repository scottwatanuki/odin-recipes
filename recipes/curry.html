Japanese Chicken Curry

Description

Japanese curry is a thick curry with a stew-like consistency and commonly includes a protein, sweet onions, carrots, and potatoes. The sauce is thickened by a roux (a mixture of fat and flour, and an addition of curry spices).

Ingredients

  • Japanese Curry Roux
  • Water
  • Chicken stock
  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Instant coffee
  • Milk
  • Oyster sauce
  • Red Wine
  • Sake
  • Soy sauce
  • Tonkatsu sauce

Steps

  1. Cut ingredients into uniform shape
  2. Cook the onion till tender and golden
  3. Cover the ingredients with just enough broth (or water)
  4. Add "Secret Ingredients" with the broth
  5. Do not forget to skim
  6. Add 2 cubes of curry roux at a time
  7. Watch out when simmering curry
  8. Make a big batch and store
