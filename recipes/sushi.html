Sushi

Description

Nigiri sushi is a variety of sushi made by layering a thin slice of fish on top of a small mound of rice. This kind of sushi is quite often made with raw fish such as salmon or tuna, but may also be made with cooked fish. Nigiri sushi is also sometimes made with eggs, shrimp or eel. Each piece of nigiri sushi is eaten whole in one bite.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Sushi Vinegar
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eel Sauce
  • Nori (Seaweed Sheet)
  • Salmon
  • Tuna
  • Shrimp (Ebi)
  • Tamago
  • Yellowtail (Hamachi)
  • Eel
  • Octopus
  • Uni

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Season the rice
  3. Prepare the topping (slice the fish, or cook the eggs, etc.)
  4. Shape the Rice
  5. Assemble
